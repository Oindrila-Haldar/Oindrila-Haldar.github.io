Metamorphosis


From slow dances and roses,
To vows,
To giggles, curls, ribbons and bows;
Behold the little princess!

From letter to words,
Words building into sentences,
To using these sentences to fool you,
The bows turned into ruffles.

From learning to compute,
To higher levels of abstraction,
The dreams now bind my hands,
The ribbons dried up as bands.
