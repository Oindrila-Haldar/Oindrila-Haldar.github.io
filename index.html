Projects taken up here:

  • Beyond The Canvas Of One: Expanding The Horizons Of Single-Image Generation

    The project implements the Enhanced SinGAN model, a robust and versatile version of the original SinGAN model (Shaham et al., ICCV, 2019). The Enhanced SinGAN is designed to be less sensitive to input image quality, enabling it to generate clean images even from noisy inputs by introducing Total Variational Loss.

  • Exploring The Impact Of Image Pre-Processing On Human Face Detection In Poor Quality Images

    Here, we explored the effect of different pre-processing techniques such as image denoising, deblurring, and contrast enhancement on face detection performance using the Haar classifier.
